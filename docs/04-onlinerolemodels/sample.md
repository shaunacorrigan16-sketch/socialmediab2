## Sample opinion piece 📝
  
**Topic: Is social media a reliable source of news?**

| **Section** | **Content** |
|---|---|
| **Title** | *Is social media a reliable source of news?* |
| **Introduction** | Social media is one of the most common ways to get news today. It is used by millions of people around the world. In my opinion, social media is not a reliable source of news. |
| **Body Paragraph** | I believe this because information on social media is not always verified before it is shared. Fake news can spread  quickly, and it can be difficult to know what is true or made up. In addition, many posts are designed to attract attention rather than to inform people. As a result, users may be influenced by misleading or fabricated information without realising it. |
| **Conclusion** | In conclusion, I do not think social media is a reliable source of news. People should be careful and check information before trusting it. |
