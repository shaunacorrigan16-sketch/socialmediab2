## 2. Reading : The Problem with Social Media
#### Read the text below. Try to mark the following sections: Introduction, main body, and conclusion.

Social media is a huge part of teenagers’ lives today. We use apps to **keep up** with friends, watch videos, and share our opinions. However, I believe social media can cause problems if we do not use it carefully.
Firstly, privacy is often overlooked. When **setting up** your profile, make it private, so only people you know in real life can see what you post. You shouldn't publish your location, or use your full name in your username, as this information could **end up** in the wrong hands.
Another problem is fake news. On social media, false information can **blow up** very quickly. You must be careful before believing everything you see. You can look up information on trusted websites to check if something is true before sharing it with others.
In conclusion, although social media can be entertaining, you should use it responsably, and if you need a break, you can always **log off**. 

## 3. Comprehension Questions

1. Is the author's opinion of social media generally positive or negative? Why?
2. Do you agree with the author's opinion?
