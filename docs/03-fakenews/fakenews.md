## Lesson Overview 📚

| **Category** | **Information** |
|---|---|
| **Level** | B2 |
| **Language** | English |
| **Aim** | *Introduce learners to the concept of **Fake News**, explain its definition, and show examples.* *Raise awareness regarding false or misleading information online.* *To develop learners' critical thinking skills regarding information and media consumption.* |

## Learning Objectives 📌


After completing this content, you will be able to:

- Define what fake news is.
- Identify common traits of fake news online and on social media.
- Express opinions about whether information is true or false.
