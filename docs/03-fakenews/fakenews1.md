## 2. What is Fake News? ❓

Fake news is a news story that is *exaggerated* or completely **made up**.

It is *false information* that is **passed off** as real.

It is often created to:

- draw attention,
- **bring down** someone's reputation,
- **cash in** through advertising.

Identifying fake news can be difficult, as articles can include:

- edited or AI-generated images,
- quotes taken out of context.

### Definition

A phrasal verb is a verb that is used in a way different from its literal meaning.

It consists of:

- a verb
- a particle (preposition or an adverb)

Some examples include:

- **to give up** *(rendirse)*
- **to find out** *(enterarse)*
- **to look after** *(cuidar a)*
