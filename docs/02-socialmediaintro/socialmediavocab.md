## 2. Reading : A Quick Overview of Social Media 📖
#### Read the text below. What verbs related to social media can you find?

**Social media networks** have **taken the world by storm**. From MySpace and Facebook of the early 2000s, to Instagram and TikTok that dominate today, their impact on society and popular culture is massive. Millions of users spend hours every day **scrolling through** their **feeds**, watching **livestreams**, sharing content, and interacting with **influencers**. These platforms have completely changed the way people communicate, consume news, and express their opinions online.
One major advantage of social media is that it allows people to stay connected regardless of distance. Friends and families can instantly exchange messages, photos, and videos through different apps and online communities. In addition, many businesses use social media **algorithms** to advertise products and reach larger audiences. However, experts also warn about the negative effects of excessive screen time, **cyberbullying**, **misinformation**, and **privacy issues**. As technology continues to evolve, social media will likely remain an important part of everyday life for future generations.
