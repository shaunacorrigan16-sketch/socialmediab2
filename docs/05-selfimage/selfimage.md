# Social Media & Self-Image 🪞

![Social media reflection](img/mirror.png)

## Lesson Overview 📚

| Category | Information |
|---|---|
| Level | B2 |
| Language | English |
| Aim | To explore how social media influences self-image, identity, and confidence. |

## Learning Objectives 📌

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:
- Talk about how social media can impact self-esteem
- Understand and use vocabulary related to identity and self-image
- Express opinions about the positive and negative effects of social media on self-image.

## 1. Discuss 💬

Discuss with your partner:
- What kinds of content do people post on social media?
- Do you think social media accurately shows "real life"?

## 2. Speaking 🗣️

In groups of 3-4, discuss the following:

"Social media negatively affects self-image and confidence"

Do you agree or disagree? Why/ why not?
Summarise your thoughts as a group in 3 lines.

## 3. Reflect 🤔
- Do you compare yourself to others on social media?
- Do you think social media affects how you feel about yourself?
